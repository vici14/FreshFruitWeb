530PM Fruits Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25 Jul 2023

Introduction

Welcome to 530PM Fruits! At 530PM Fruits, we value your privacy and strive to protect your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using our services.

Information We Collect

When you use our online shop, we may collect the following types of information:

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us during the ordering process, such as your name, phone number, and delivery address.

2.2 Geolocation Information

To enhance the user experience and provide efficient delivery services, we may collect and use geolocation information from your device. Geolocation data allows us to determine your approximate location, which helps us offer personalized delivery options and estimate delivery times accurately.

2.2.1 Consent for Geolocation Data

By using our services and providing consent to access your device's geolocation data, you agree to our collection, use, and processing of this information for the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy. You can grant or revoke this consent at any time through your device settings.

2.2.2 Data Sharing

We do not share your geolocation data with third parties unless it is necessary for delivery purposes. In such cases, we will share the minimum amount of data required to complete the delivery process.

2.3 Payment Information

If you choose to make a payment through your banking app or using Momo, we will not collect or store your banking or financial information. Payments will be processed through the respective payment providers, and their privacy policies will govern the use of your payment information.

2.4 Order Information

We may collect details of the products you purchase, order history, and any communications related to your orders.

2.5 Log Data

When you visit our website, our servers automatically record certain log data, which may include your IP address, browser type, device information, and the pages you visit.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders and provide customer support.
  • Improve our services, website functionality, and user experience.
  • Send you relevant updates, offers, and promotional materials, if you opt-in to receive such communications.
  • Monitor and analyze website usage, trends, and activities to enhance our services.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We use Firebase, a secure and reliable platform, for authentication and data storage. However, please note that no method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Your Choices

Communication Preferences: You may choose to receive promotional emails from us by opting-in during the checkout process. You can opt-out of these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe link provided in the emails or by contacting us directly.

Access and Update Your Information: If you wish to access, update, or delete the personal information we have about you, please contact us at 530pmfruits@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with their personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take appropriate steps to remove the information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices and services. The updated version will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be revised accordingly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the way we handle your information, please contact us at 530pmfruits@gmail.com.

By using 530PM Fruits, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Thank you for trusting us with your information!
